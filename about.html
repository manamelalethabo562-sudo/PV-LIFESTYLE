Who We Are

PV Lifestyle Restaurant and Bar offers delicious food in a choice of your setting — whether in our elegant Private room or the relaxed Outside Chillas area.

We pride ourselves on providing an intimate atmosphere perfect for fine dining, celebrations, or a quiet evening out. Our bar features a wide range of Spirits, Whiskey, Vodka, Cognac, and more — carefully selected to complement every meal.

Beyond the restaurant, we proudly offer catering services and host various events, bringing the same quality and elegance to your special occasions.

At PV Lifestyle, every detail is crafted to create unforgettable experiences — from the freshest ingredients to impeccable service in the heart of Mokopane.
